Communication, cultural management and artistic creation at the service of narratives that connect.

Stefanía Ramírez G.

I am a strategic communicator, cultural manager and creator. For over ten years I have worked at the intersection of institutions, communities and artistic practices. I lead communications at Fundación Alejandro Ángel Escobar and previously worked as digital communicator at the Museum of Modern Art in Medellín; I have produced cultural events with international artists; designed positioning strategies for scientific and philanthropic organizations, and researched how narratives give meaning to collective experience.

Between 2018 and 2023 I lived in East Africa (Uganda, Kenya and Tanzania). I taught Spanish as an additional language, did fieldwork on a solar energy project, accompanied educational communities and developed a self-taught creative practice. Those years are not a parenthesis in my trajectory: they explain why I can operate with rigor in uncertain contexts, with audiences that don't share my references, with resources that fall short and deadlines that don't wait.

Today, from Bogotá, I combine that journey with an artistic practice centered on handmade paper, collage, artist books and the documentation of memories. I don't separate it from my strategic work because they are not different things: in both cases it is about giving form to knowledge.

Let's work together

Asante sana is Swahili. It means thank you very much. I learned it in East Africa, in the markets of Kampala, in the classrooms of the École Française, on the roads of northern Uganda. There I understood that gratitude is not a gesture of courtesy: it is a form of sustained attention. A way of recognizing that what happens —the encounters, the conversations, the materials one works with, the stories one listens to— has value before being processed, communicated or archived.

That principle runs through everything I do: institutional and artistic work, processes with communities and individuals, strategy documents and handmade books.
