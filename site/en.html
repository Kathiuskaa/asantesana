Communication, cultural management and artistic creation at the service of narratives that connect.

Stefanía Ramírez G.

I am a communicator, cultural manager and content producer. My work focuses on building narratives rooted in the real that connect with those who receive them.

I accompany artists and cultural agents in designing communication strategies that make their work visible and put it in dialogue with their audiences.

My current practice revolves around collage with plant-based papers, the creation of artist books, and the experience of being a woman. Travel has broadened my perspective and integrated a more open and diverse vision of art and its possibilities.
